SUNAQ represents 20 years of expertise in our tech-field and with our focus on the environmental challenges in the ocean-space we believe we can contribute to wave-breaking global solutions.

The key problem our floating solar solution solves is to reduce the amount of climate gas emissions and sound pollution from diesel barge engines used to power equipment for aquaculture and fish farming operations. Currently, most barges operate on diesel generators that produce significant greenhouse gas emissions like CO2 and NOx.


By installing floating solar PV system on barges and feeding the energy into batteries, together with batteries we eliminate the need for polluting diesel generators by up to 75%.

Specifically, our floating solar PV systems allow fish farm operators to:


  • Reduce diesel fuel usage and emissions from generator
  • Lower energy costs by utilizing solar energy
  • Provide clean, renewable energy to power equipment
  • Improve sustainability and meet environmental goals
  • Reduce noise in the workplace to improve conditions

Our Product

Our product will bring a great benefit to our chosen customer segment. Diesel-driven feeding barges are used by almost 100% of all Norwegian-controlled salmon and trout producers and the use of automated feeding barges is increasing globally.


A new development in the direction of sustainability has been the introduction of batteries in the barges with highly sophisticated BMI and control over running- and charging time from the diesel gen set. This opens up the use of solar PV and other solutions to help reduce diesel consumption.

Our solution is:


  • Self-cleaning and winter safe < 6°C sea water temperature
  • Delivers clean energy
  • The cooling effect from the sea gives a higher yield
  • Storm and hurricane-proof solution
  • Re-use old fish pens
  • Solid waterproof solarpanels

Technology

We push our partners to comply with the solutions we need to succeed with our product. This is called innovation - And it drives technology and humanity forward. There is no doubt about it. SUB Solar power harvesting MUST be a part of the global solution.

As the name indicates, the SUB Solar™ concept is based on a unique solution. We submerge our uniquely designed solar panels in water on a special floating structure.

This has benefits like:


  • Cooling of the panels (10-15% more energy).
  • Self-cleaning.
  • Snow and frost-safe (< 6°c sea temperature in winter)
  • Structural stability.

Nature supplies us with water from above. The movement of the ocean keeps our panels from being soiled by particles like pollen or bird droppings. Changing, circulating, and treating the water is all that's needed, and our new system design handles this on its own.

The technology is based on the idea of a freshwater basin for the aquaculture industry. The pool keeps our waterproof solar panels in a controlled environment. We use the toughest solar panels in the world.
